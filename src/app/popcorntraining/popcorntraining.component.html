Popcorn Training

Details











I joined Popcorn Training in May 2017 as a contractor, doing mostly front-end and visual work on their product in Ruby on Rails. As a remote developer, I gained a lot of experience in working on my own while collaborating with a geographically dispersed team.

I learned how to analyse and interpret user requirements and to design solutions that are practical, meet user needs and in some cases also comply with legal requirements. I also learned how to acclimatize myself to an existing codebase with lots of complexity and moving parts.

I left Popcorn Training in August 2017 after I moved to Berlin and could no longer commit to the hours necessary to work for them.
