Videogames

Ever since I got my first computer at the age of 9, I've been captivated with the myriad of fantastic worlds and stories to be found in videogames. The very first game I ever played was called 3 Skulls of the Toltecs, a little known point-and-click adventure set in 1866 Arizona, following the (misadventures) of a goofy, but lovable cowboy called Fenimore Filmore as he attempts to uncover the mystery of an ancient treasure.

From there my love for videogames only grew and today I love to play a myriad of different genres, from competitive shooters to complex strategy and 4X games. I love to keep up with gaming news and can often be found discussing new games or industry developments on online forums.
