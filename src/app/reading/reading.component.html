Reading

Probably going hand-in-hand with my love for videogames, I've been reading since the day I learned to read. I fell in love with the worlds and characters that books brought to life in my imagination. From Tintin to Harry Potter and even the most obscure crime novels, I read anything I could get my hands on.

Many a night in my childhood was spent reading books under the covers with a flashlight after my bedtime. Holidays would often see me reading my books on the couch instead of playing outdoors with my peers.

Although I don't have as much time to read anymore, I still greatly enjoy getting engrossed in a book from time to time.
